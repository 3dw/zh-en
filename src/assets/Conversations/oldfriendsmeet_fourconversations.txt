四種對白選項：
正確對白：

A: "It's been so long! How have you been?"
B: "I missed you so much! Let's catch up!"

錯誤對白 1：

A: "Why are you here? I thought we were meeting online."
B: "Oh, I just wanted to surprise you."

錯誤對白 2：

A: "Do I know you? You look familiar."
B: "Yes, we went to the same school for years!"

錯誤對白 3：

A: "I thought you moved abroad!"
B: "No, I’ve been living next door to you all this time."
