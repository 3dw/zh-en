四種對白選項：

正確對白：

A: "You never listen to me anymore!"
B: "I do listen, but you don't give me a chance to explain!"

錯誤對白 1：

A: "Why did you eat my dessert?"
B: "I didn’t know it was yours, the fridge was empty!"

錯誤對白 2：

A: "Let’s break up! I can’t stand your cooking."
B: "Fine! I was tired of you complaining anyway."

錯誤對白 3：

A: "You forgot my birthday again!"
B: "No, I just wanted to surprise you tomorrow."
